Chipotle Rice

Simple imitation recipe of Chipotle's popular cilantro lime rice

Ingredients: 2 cups basmati rice unrinsed, or any long-grained white rice, rinsed. 1 bay leaf. salt. 1 teaspoon olive oil or rice brain oil, optional. 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro minced. 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice from 1-2 limes. 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice from 1-2 lemons.
