Guacamole Recipe

A copycat version of Chipotle's popular Guacamole:

Prep time is 5 mins. Cook time is 2 mins. Total time is 7 mins. Ingredients: 2 Large Avocados halved and pitted. 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice 1 teaspon fresh lime juice. 1/4 cup red onion finely chopped. 1/2 jalapeno pepper stemmed, seeded, and finely chopped. 2 tablespoons cilantro leaves finely chopped. Salt Tortilla chips for serving.
