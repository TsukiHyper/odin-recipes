Chipotle Lime Tortilla Chips

An imitation recipe of Chipotle's popular lime Tortilla Chips

20 corn tortillas, yellow or white corn measuring 5 1/2 inches in diameter. 2 tablespoons kosher or margarita salt. 1/4 cup lime juice, freshly squeezed. 3 cups deep frying oil, such as sunflower, peanut, or avocado.
